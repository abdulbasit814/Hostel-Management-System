About Us

The UDS Nyankpala Campus Hostel Manager System — your go-to platform for finding and managing hostel accommodation with ease and confidence. Whether you're looking for a campus-managed or private hostel, you’ll get verified information, real-time room updates, and full details before you book.

We've made it simple to handle everything from online payments to tracking how close your hostel is to campus.
